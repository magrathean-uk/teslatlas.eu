Teslatlas
Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 May 2026

Teslatlas is a local-first app for viewing and analysing TeslaMate data through direct PostgreSQL or a MyTeslaMate/TeslaMate API endpoint that you configure. It mirrors data into local storage for route replay, battery-health estimates, charge diagnostics, parked-drain insight, cost tracking, prepared maps, and offline-first analysis.

The important bit: Magrathean does not operate a cloud relay for your vehicle data. Vehicle data is read from the data source you configure and stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or send material.

Controller

MAGRATHEAN UK LTD., a company registered in England and Wales (Company No. 16955343) with registered office at 16 Caledonian Court, West Street, Watford, England, WD17 1RY is the controller for personal data we process for the Teslatlas website, App Store administration, support, security, and customer communications.

For data inside your TeslaMate database, MyTeslaMate account, API endpoint, Cloudflare Access proxy, local network, or vehicle-history source, you or the relevant service/operator are normally the controller. Tesla, Apple, MyTeslaMate, Cloudflare, hosting providers, and other services you choose are separate providers/controllers for their own processing.

Data processed locally by the app

Teslatlas may process and store on your device:

  • connection profiles and endpoint configuration;
  • PostgreSQL host, port, database name, username, connection mode, and related settings;
  • MyTeslaMate/TeslaMate API endpoint, login, password/token, bearer token, basic auth details, Cloudflare Access credentials, custom proxy headers, and similar auth material;
  • vehicle identifiers and metadata, including car names and VIN where present in the source;
  • drives, route samples, charges, charge samples, odometer, state-of-charge, temperature, speed, location, timestamps, costs, efficiency, charging, and parked-drain data from the configured source;
  • computed predictions, route optimisations, prepared map artifacts, one-year/long-range route caches, and analysis outputs;
  • app preferences, onboarding state, sync state, row counts, freshness indicators, confidence labels, and local diagnostics;
  • exported backups, support bundles, or public share files if you create them.

Credentials are stored in Apple Keychain or platform-protected storage. Vehicle data, local mirror data, computed predictions, and map artifacts are stored locally on the device.

Data we do not collect from the app

Magrathean does not collect app analytics, behavioural tracking, advertising identifiers, location history, vehicle history, driving history, VIN, credentials, TeslaMate data, MyTeslaMate data, or usage history from the app.

Teslatlas does not send Tesla commands, does not write back to TeslaMate, and does not route vehicle data through Magrathean infrastructure by default. We do not sell personal data.

Network communication

Teslatlas may connect to:

  • the TeslaMate PostgreSQL database you configure;
  • the MyTeslaMate/TeslaMate API endpoint you configure;
  • a Cloudflare Access proxy or custom proxy endpoint you configure;
  • Apple services for App Store distribution, StoreKit subscriptions, restore purchases, updates, platform crash reporting where handled by the operating system, and platform operation;
  • Magrathean websites only when you open legal, support, release-notes, or product links;
  • support channels only when you deliberately contact us or send material.

The data source, network owner, cloud host, Cloudflare, Apple, Tesla, MyTeslaMate, or other third parties you choose may process data under their own terms. Magrathean does not receive your vehicle data unless you send it to us.

StoreKit and subscription state

Teslatlas may use Apple's StoreKit to check subscription products, trial status, entitlement state, renewal dates, cached entitlement state, restoration, transaction updates, and payment status. Apple manages App Store billing and may process Apple ID, purchase, tax, region, payment, and device data under Apple's terms. Magrathean does not receive your full payment-card details from Apple.

Exports and support bundles

Teslatlas may let you export backups, support bundles, public share files, screenshots, or diagnostics. Some export paths can redact VIN, time, and location. You are responsible for checking exported files before sharing them.

If you send us support material, we process what you provide so we can respond, troubleshoot, protect the product, and keep records. Do not send VIN, exact locations, credentials, Cloudflare tokens, database passwords, vehicle data, or regulated data unless we have agreed a secure route and it is necessary.

Legal bases

For personal data we process as controller, we rely on:

  • contract where processing is necessary to provide requested app, support, licensing, or App Store-related services;
  • legitimate interests to operate, secure, improve, document, defend, and support Teslatlas;
  • consent where required for optional communications;
  • legal obligation where we must keep records or respond to lawful requests.

Local vehicle-data processing happens on your device for the functionality you requested. Where you are not the sole data subject, you are responsible for having rights and authority to process the vehicle data.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data and do not use app data for advertising.

We may use service providers for email, hosting, security, issue tracking, legal, accounting, App Store administration, and operational support. Where a provider processes personal data for us, we use appropriate contractual controls.

Vehicle data is shared with Magrathean only if you deliberately send it to us.

International transfers

We primarily operate from the United Kingdom. Apple, support, hosting, email, security, and other providers may process personal data outside the UK or EEA. Where required, we use appropriate safeguards such as adequacy regulations or standard contractual clauses.

Connections you initiate to TeslaMate, MyTeslaMate, Cloudflare, or other endpoints occur directly from your device to those destinations and are governed by those providers/operators.

You can contact us for more information about the safeguards used for a relevant international transfer and, where applicable, how to obtain a copy of those safeguards.

Retention

Local app data remains on your device until you clear it, remove connection profiles, delete caches, delete exports, uninstall the app, or the operating system removes it.

StoreKit purchase and entitlement records are handled by Apple under Apple's terms. We keep support, security, business, and communication records only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, legal compliance, dispute handling, and auditability.

For controller records we hold, retention depends on the type of record and the risk involved. Support and customer communications are kept only while needed to answer the request, maintain the relationship, handle disputes, or preserve auditability. Security records are kept for investigation, defence, and abuse-prevention periods. Accounting, tax, company, contract, licensing, and business records are kept for the period required by law or for ordinary limitation periods, normally up to six years where relevant. We delete or anonymise records when they are no longer needed.

Security

Teslatlas is designed around local storage, Apple Keychain for credentials, direct connections to user-configured endpoints, no Magrathean vehicle-data relay, no write-back to TeslaMate, and no Tesla command surface.

No method of storage or transmission is completely secure. You remain responsible for securing your device, Apple ID, TeslaMate database, MyTeslaMate endpoint, Cloudflare Access configuration, backups, exports, support bundles, local network, and credentials.

App Tracking Transparency

Teslatlas does not track your activity across other companies' apps or websites for advertising or data-broker purposes and does not request Apple's App Tracking Transparency permission.

Teslatlas is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children through the app.

Product findings, predictions, warnings, diagnostics, audit outputs, quota calculations, and operational suggestions are informational outputs for human review and action.
