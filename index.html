Short answers.

The important limits are simple.

Is Teslatlas affiliated with Tesla?

No. Tesla, TeslaMate, and MyTeslaMate are separate third parties.

Does it control the car?

No. Teslatlas is a viewer, not a vehicle control app.

Does Magrathean receive vehicle data or crash diagnostics?

No. Teslatlas does not send Magrathean vehicle data, analytics, or automatic crash diagnostics. Send support details only when you choose to.

Can I rely on battery estimates?

No. Treat them as informational only.

Make TeslaMate easier to read.

Install Teslatlas from the App Store and connect your own source.

Teslatlas is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Tesla, TeslaMate, or MyTeslaMate. Battery, charging, efficiency, and degradation outputs are informational only.
